How to play

Type in commands whenever it is your turn. Commands like the following:

player.moveLeft();
player.moveDown();
player.checkForCheese();

What is programming?

You see, computers do not understand our language. In order to talk to them, we have to use a language that computer can read. This is called programming. Programming is like telling the computer to solve a jigsaw puzzle, complete a rubix cube, or play a game of checkers. It is like combining the human brain and the computer brain to make something awesome and cool.

Where to learn coding?

There are many other fun and interactive online coding games you can check out, such as Code Combat and Code Monkey. Also be sure to check out your local library for coding events and classes.

What is a hackathon?

A Hackathon is a gathering of people interested in coding. Together, they compete in teams for awesome prizes and swag. It is one of the best places to make friends and to sharpen your coding skill, even if you never coded before. A lot of people comes to a hackathon just to learn new things and exchange new ideas.
